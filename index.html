About GDG IGDTUW

Here at GDG IGDTUW, we explore technology and use it to build products that brings about positive change in society. We conduct mentorship sessions, workshops, knowledge transfer sessions, hackathons, coding contests, open source programs, etc. where students from across the globe get to showcase their knowledge and develop their skillset.

We have helped more than 7000+ university students to expand their technical knowledge and skills.

About Devcation`25

Devcation is a week-long technical festival organized by Google Developer Groups - Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women that celebrates diversity in the TechForGood Domain. During the week, we have the Devcation`25 hackathon. These events are designed to spark your creativity, encourage innovative thinking, and help you craft tech solutions that stand out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can contribute?

Devcation is open to anyone with an interest in technology, innovation, and problem-solving. Participants can include students, developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and professionals from various backgrounds.

How do I register for the event?

You can register through our website by clicking on the "Register" button and filling out the required information. Registration is free and open until the event start date.

What should I bring to the event?

Please bring your laptop, charger, and any other devices you might need for development. For in-person events, we recommend bringing a water bottle and comfortable clothing.

Do I need to have a team before joining?

No, you don't need to have a team beforehand. We'll have team formation sessions at the beginning of the event to help participants form teams based on skills and interests.

What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical complexity, design, usability, and potential impact. The exact criteria will be shared with all participants at the start of the event.

Is there any cost to participate?

The event is completely free to attend. Food and refreshments will be provided for in-person events, and we'll have plenty of resources available for all participants.

What are the team size requirements?

Teams can consist of 2-5 members. We encourage diverse teams with a mix of skills and backgrounds for the best experience.

Can I participate remotely?

Yes, Devcation offers both in-person and remote participation options. All events will be accessible online for remote participants.

What if I'm new to hackathons?

Devcation is beginner-friendly! We'll have workshops, mentors, and resources specifically designed for first-time participants. It's a great opportunity to learn and grow.

How can I prepare for the event?

Brush up on your technical skills, brainstorm project ideas, and make sure your development environment is set up. We'll send out a preparation guide to all registered participants.
